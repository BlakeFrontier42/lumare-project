## What Lumare Becomes

Lumare is not a trading app. It's not a robo-advisor. It's a **Capital Intelligence OS** — the decision layer that sits above every brokerage account, bank account, and asset class.

### The Hybrid
- A **robo-advisor** (automated portfolio construction)
- A **hedge fund strategy replicator** (institutional-style allocation)
- A **macro intelligence terminal** (Bloomberg for everyone)
- An **autonomous execution engine** (algorithmic trading)
- A **financial planning suite** (all-inclusive wealth management)
